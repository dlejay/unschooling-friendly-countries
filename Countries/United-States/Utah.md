# Unschooling in Utah

## Compulsory education

School-age children in Utah must receive an education from age 6 to 18 (inclusive).

## Unschooling

According to CRHE :

> **Homeschool statute:** Parents must file a one-time notice with their local school district. There are no parent qualification, bookkeeping, instruction subject, or assessment requirements.

## Statistics

Utah no longer tracks official homeschool enrollment (since a 2017 law ended annual reporting).
