#Unschooling in Arkansas

## Compulsory education

Arkansas’s compulsory education/attendance rules apply to children age 5 through 17 (with the cut-off tied to August 1).

## Unschooling

According to Arkansas Code Title 6. Education § 6-15-503,

> (a)(1) Parents or legal guardians desiring to provide a home school for their children shall give written notice to the superintendent of their local school district of their intent to provide a home school for their children and agree that the parent or legal guardian is responsible for the education of his or her children during the time the parent or legal guardian provides a home school for the children: […]
