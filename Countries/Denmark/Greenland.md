# Unschooling in Greenland

## Compulsory education

According to the Self-government's statutory announcement of Inatsisartutlov about primary school,

> **Chapter 6**
>
> _Obligation to provide education and parental rights and duties_
>
> _Obligation to provide training_
>
> **§ 29.**  Any child living in Greenland or who has to stay in the country for at least 6 months is subject to education in accordance with the rules of sections 30-31.

> _Entry and termination of the compulsory education_
>
> **§ 31.**  Without prejudice to section 18 (2), the compulsory education shall take place from the beginning of the school year in the calendar year in which the child reaches the age of 6 and shall cease after the child has received regular education for 10 years. 4th
> 
> _PCS. 2nd_ The Municipal Board may, at the request of the parents or with their consent, approve that the commencement of a child's schooling be postponed to one year after the entry of the compulsory education, when justified by the child's development.
> 
> _PCS. 3._  At the request of the parents, the municipal council may exempt a student from teaching after 9 years of teaching when special reasons speak for it. Exemption from education after 9 years of education requires the implementation of a vocational and training course for that student.

## Unschooling

> _Content of the compulsory education_
>
> **§ 30.** The compulsory education entails the right and duty to participate in the teaching of the folkeskole or in other instruction that measures up to what is normally required in the folkeskole.
> 
> _PCS. 2._ Naalakkersuisut shall lay down rules for measures to ensure the completion of the education, as well as for the school’s supervision of pupils during school hours.
> 
> _PCS. 3._ Children who receive home instruction or other instruction that measures up to what is normally required in the folkeskole are not required to participate in the folkeskole’s teaching.

> _Parental responsibility_
> 
> **§ 33.** The parents, cf. § 34, are responsible for ensuring that the child fulfils the compulsory education and must not place obstacles in the way of this.
> 
> _PCS. 2._ Schooling outside the folkeskole must be notified to the municipal council in the municipality where the pupil resides.
