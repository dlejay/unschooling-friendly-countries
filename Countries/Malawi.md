# Unschooling in Malawi

## Compulsory schooling

According to the Education Act (2013) :

> **13.** The provision of primary education in Government schools shall be free of tuitions to all and compulsory for every child below eighteen years of age.

This law applies to all children resident in Malawi.

According to the National Education Policy (2013) :

> The primary cycle is for eight years, from standard 1 to standard 8.
