# Unschooling in Andorra

## Compulsory schooling

Article 4 of the _Llei qualificada d’educació, de 3-9-93_ states :

> Basic education is compulsory and free for all
> citizens, natural persons and legal foreigners
> residents, at the levels established by article 7 of the
> this Law. The State guarantees this right through the
> public centers.

> […]

> **Article 7**
>
> 1. Compulsory schooling includes the primary and secondary education levels. It runs from 6 years of age to 16 years of age.
> 2. Every child has the right to be enrolled in school from 3 years of age, if the parents or guardian so request.
> 3. Students may remain enrolled in school until 18 years of age in order to pursue the secondary education level.
