# Unschooling in the Islamic Republic of Mauritania

## Compulsory education

According to the _Loi n°2022-023/ portant loi d’orientation
du système éducatif national_ :

> **Article 3**
>
> Education is an absolute national priority, and education is compulsory from six to fifteen years of age.

## Compulsory schooling

According to the _Ordonnance n°2005-015 portant protection pénale de l’enfant_ :

> **Article 36**
>
> […]
>
> Violations of the school-enrolment requirement must be reported to the academic inspectorate by municipal councillors, members of the teaching staff, social workers, non-governmental organizations working with children, public-authority officers, and any other person who becomes aware of them.
