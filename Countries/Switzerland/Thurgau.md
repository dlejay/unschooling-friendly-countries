# Unschooling in Thurgau

## Compulsory education

The Volksschulgesetz (RB 411.11) establishes a compulsory *Volksschule* comprising Kindergarten, Primarschule and Sekundarschule (§ 1).

The *Schulpflicht* begins with the school year following a child’s fourth birthday — reference date 31 July — and lasts eleven years (two years of Kindergarten, six of Primarschule, three of Sekundarstufe I) ; parents may declare a one-year deferral of entry (§ 37 Abs. 1).

## Home education

Compulsory schooling may be discharged in a public school, an authorised private school, or — per the *Richtlinie für den Privatunterricht* — through authorised *Privatunterricht* at home by a privately engaged teacher who is entitled to teach at a public school in the canton of Thurgau, following the cantonal curriculum.
