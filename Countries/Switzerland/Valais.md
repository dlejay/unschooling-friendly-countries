# Unschooling in Valais

## Compulsory education

In Valais, compulsory education lasts 11 years.

## Home education

According to the _Directives_ in Valais :

> **Art 2.** Goals
>
> Homeschooling must enable the child to acquire, at the end of compulsory schooling,
> knowledge and skills similar to those provided for in the study plans in force in the public school.
>
> […]
>
> **Art 7.** Basic training
>
> The person in charge of home schooling must be in possession of a recognized pedagogical training […]

Valais does not create separate rules for foreign nationals ; all children residing in Valais are bound by the same laws.

Pupils taught at home sit the cantonal end-of-cycle examinations (4H, 8H, 11CO) at a nearby public school.

## Pending legislation

In December 2025 the Conseil d'État adopted and transmitted to the Grand Conseil a package of school-law reforms, including a new _Loi sur l'enseignement privé_ (LEPriv). The bill would give private education, including home education, a dedicated and more detailed statutory framework, whereas the current regime is based on provisions of the existing school legislation supplemented by departmental directives. It passed first reading in the Grand Conseil in May 2026 ; a second reading is pending. The canton presents the reform as formalising and clarifying the existing framework rather than liberalising home education. Recheck and re-source on enactment.
