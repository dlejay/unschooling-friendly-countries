# Unschooling in Schwyz

## Compulsory schooling

In Canton Schwyz, the mandatory duration of schooling (*Schulpflicht*) covers nine years of formal primary and lower secondary education, preceded by one obligatory year of Kindergarten (the second of two Kindergarten years ; the first remains voluntary). The total period of compulsory education is therefore ten years (1 obligatory Kindergarten year + 9 school years). The school year runs from 1 August to 31 July, anchored in § 23 of the Volksschulgesetz (SRSZ 611.210).

## Privatunterricht — current legal framework
 
Schwyz allows the *Schulpflicht* to be discharged through *Privatunterricht* (private tuition at home), subject to a permission regime under the *Weisungen zur Führung von privaten Volksschulen* of 1 February 2006 (SRSZ 618.111). The text in force is the consolidated version dated 1 February 2022, which integrates seven amendments through 26 April 2021. The original 2006 PDF still hosted on `sz.ch/public/upload/assets/4027/618.111.pdf` is **not** the current text and should not be relied upon — the consolidated version on lexfind should be used instead.
 
The relevant provisions are :
 
> **§ 3b c) Privatunterricht.** Für die Erteilung der Bewilligung zum Besuch von Privatunterricht ist dem Amt für Volksschulen und Sport mindestens ein halbes Jahr vor Beginn des Privatunterrichts ein begründetes schriftliches Gesuch mit einem Unterrichtsprogramm sowie Angaben zur unterrichtenden Person, zu den Schulungsräumen und zum Stundenplan einzureichen.
 
> **§ 4d Abs. 2 Ausnahmen.** Privatunterricht ist von der Erfüllung der Bedingungen teilweise ausgenommen. Verbindlich sind die Gewährleistung des Übertritts in die öffentliche Schule, das Ausstellen von Zeugnissen und die Vorgaben betreffend Ausbildung der unterrichtenden Lehrperson. Der kantonale Lehrplan ist wegleitend.
 
In practice, this means :
 
- **Bewilligungspflicht.** Privatunterricht requires advance permission from the *Amt für Volksschulen und Sport* (AVS). It is not merely *meldepflichtig* (notice-based) as in some other cantons.
- **Six-month lead time.** The reasoned application must be filed at least six months before the intended start.
- **Teaching qualification.** Under § 4a Abs. 2, teaching staff must hold a teaching diploma recognised by the Erziehungsrat for the relevant Volksschule level. Under § 4d Abs. 2 this rule is one of the few that remains binding for Privatunterricht (alongside the Übertritt guarantee and the duty to issue Zeugnisse).
- **Cantonal Lehrplan is «wegleitend»** (guiding), not strictly binding — but the transfer back into the public system must remain feasible at any time, which in practice constrains pedagogical freedom.
- **Supervision.** Privatunterricht is overseen by the Abteilung Schulcontrolling within AVS (§ 5 Abs. 2). Permission can be withdrawn for non-compliance (§ 5 Abs. 5).

The combination of these requirements means that genuinely curriculum-free unschooling is not realistically compatible with the current Schwyz regime, even before the 2024 tightening.
 
## 2024 revision (adopted ; Inkrafttreten to confirm)
 
The Erziehungsrat conducted a *Teilrevision der Weisungen zur Führung von privaten Volksschulen* in two readings :
 
- **First reading :** 24 June 2024.
- **Second reading and adoption :** ERB Nr. 50 of 12 December 2024 («Teilrevision der Weisungen zur Führung von privaten Volksschulen, 2. Lesung»). The Erziehungsrat adopted the amendments and ordered their publication in the cantonal Amtsblatt.
Substantively, the revision tightens the regime for Privatunterricht and Privatschulen by :
 
- restricting Privatunterricht to the parents’ own children and/or children living in the same household;
- imposing a minimum duration of six months per Privatunterricht permission ;
- aligning the start of every Privatunterricht permission with the start of the school year on 1 August ;
- requiring at least one appropriately qualified teacher per *Schulstufe* (Kindergarten / Primar / Sek I).

## Institutional context (April 2026)
 
In April 2026 the Schwyzer Kantonsrat supported the abolition of the Erziehungsrat, with implementation envisaged for the new legislature beginning in 2028. The Regierungsrat must now prepare the necessary legislative changes.
