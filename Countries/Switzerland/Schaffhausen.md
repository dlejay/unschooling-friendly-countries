# Unschooling in Schaffhausen
## Compulsory education
In Canton Schaffhausen, compulsory schooling covers 11 years : two years of Kindergarten, six years of primary school, and three years of lower secondary school.
## Home education
Schaffhausen allows home education only within a restrictive permission regime.
Long-term private instruction (*privater Unterricht*) applies where children living in the same household are taught privately for more than six months. Since the 2023 reform, this requires authorisation and the teaching person must hold an EDK-recognised teaching diploma.
A separate category of temporary private instruction (*vorübergehender privater Unterricht*) allows families to teach their children privately without a teaching diploma for limited periods : twice during compulsory schooling, for a maximum of six months each, including school holidays. This is designed for situations such as longer foreign stays, sabbaticals or work-related stays abroad, not as an open-ended homeschooling regime.
Private instruction must meet the educational goals of the public schools and is subject to cantonal supervision. It is therefore not compatible with curriculum-free unschooling.
## Statistics

| ----------- | ---------------------- |
| 2022/23     | 50                     |
| 2023/24     | 7                      |
The sharp fall is reported by Avenir Suisse and attributed to the new teaching-diploma requirement from the 2023/24 school year.
## News
On 12 March 2023, Schaffhausen voters approved the school-law revision on private schools and private instruction by 60.1 % Yes. The reform followed a period in which the canton could no longer require a teaching diploma because the requirement lacked a sufficient statutory basis. The revised law placed the diploma requirement into the school law itself.
