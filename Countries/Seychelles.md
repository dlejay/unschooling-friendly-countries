# Unschooling in Seychelles

## Compulsory schooling

According to the Education Act,

> 49. (1) Every child shall attend school from the beginning of the school calendar in the school year until the child has completed the compulsory school age.


> 57. It is the duty of the parent of every child of compulsory school age to cause the child to attend school regularly.

This applies to all residents.
No legal framework allows home education or homeschooling exemptions.
