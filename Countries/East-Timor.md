# Unschooling in East Timor

## Compulsory schooling

According to Education system framework law,

> **Article 58 Compulsory Schooling**
>
> 1. The system of nine years of compulsory schooling outlined in this law shall apply to
> students who enrolled in the first year of the first cycle of primary education beginning the
> academic year 2008-2009 onwards.
