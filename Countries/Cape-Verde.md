# Unschooling in Cape Verde

## Compulsory education

According to the Decreto-Legislativo nº 2/2010,

> **Article 13
> Compulsory Nature**
>
> 1.	The State guarantees compulsory and universal education up to the 10th year of schooling.
> 2.	The State promotes the creation of conditions to extend compulsory schooling up to the 12th year of schooling.

## Homeschooling

Homeschooling is officially recognized by Decreto-Lei nº 32/2007 :

> **Article 6
> Exclusion**
> 	1.	The following forms of education are excluded from the scope of application of this decree:
> 
> a) Individual and home education;
> b) Religious education;
> c) Educational establishments of foreign States in which programmes not approved by the Ministry of Education are taught;
> d) Training schools for political parties or other political or religious organisations;
> e) Higher education institutions;
> f) Social activities carried out by youth centres, Municipal Councils and Non-Governmental Organisations (NGOs);
> g) Establishments providing intensive instruction subject to specific regulation, or simple training in any technique or art, practical language instruction, vocational training, or cultural extension activities.
>
> 	2.	For the purposes of paragraph (a) of the previous number:
> 
> a) Individual education: education provided by one or more teachers to no more than ten students outside an educational establishment;
> 
> b) Home education: education provided at the student’s domicile by a family member, a person living with the student, or a teacher.
