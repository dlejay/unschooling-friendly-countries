# Unschooling in Burkina Faso

## Compulsory education

According to the _Loi n°013-2007/AN_ :

> **Article 4**
>
> Basic education is compulsory for all children between 6 and 16 years old.
>

## Unschooling

According to the _Loi n°013-2007/AN_ basic education is to be delivered at school.

The 2019–2024 strategy paper _Stratégie nationale de scolarisation des élèves des zones à forts défis sécuritaires (SSEZDS)_ — published by the Ministère — repeatedly uses the word
“scolarisation” (i.e. school attendance) as the referent to the national “obligation” to provide schooling for displaced/affected children.

A research article from Jean-Claude Bationa and Innocent Kiemdé from 2023 also talks about compulsory schooling.
